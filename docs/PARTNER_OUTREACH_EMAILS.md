Placeholders: `{{FirstName}}`, `{{FuneralHomeName}}`.

> **Angle (SES-053): the courtesy, not a commission.** The partner is offered a

## How to send

1. Personalize the first line of Touch 1 with one specific detail about their business (their town, a service you saw on their site) before anything else.
2. Send 10–15 per day max from hello@wordsbywtm.com, spread through the morning; more than that risks the domain's deliverability.
3. Any reply, even a "not interested," gets a short human answer within a day; interested ones get their `?ref=` link plus the card page URL (wordsbywtm.com/partners/card?code=their-token) in the first response.
4. **Never** frame the courtesy as money to the partner. It's a discount the *family* receives; the partner arranges it. No commission, no revenue share, nothing to settle.
