# Partner Program — Cold Outreach Target List (wordsbywtm.com)

**Built:** 2026-07-02 · **Targets:** 61 (44 funeral homes, 9 hospices, 8 celebrants) · **Tiers:** A 16 / B 40 / C 5

## How this list was built

Candidates were found via web search ("family-owned funeral home + grief resources", "hospice bereavement coordinator email", "certified funeral celebrant contact") and each organization's own website was then fetched directly. An email is included **only if it literally appears on the organization's own public site** (contact / staff / grief-support page) — no guessed address patterns, no data-broker or directory sites. The Source URL column is the exact page where the email appears. Corporate-chain locations (SCI/Dignity Memorial, StoneMor, Foundation Partners, Carriage, and national hospice chains) were skipped in favor of independents; preference was given to organizations with a grief-resources/aftercare page (they already curate resources for families — the natural fit and a genuine first line). **Caveats on freshness:** all emails were seen live on 2026-07-02, but a few are legacy mailboxes worth bounce-checking (Haren-Wood @msn.com, Cattermole @aol.com, Pitman @hotmail.com, T.V. Williams / Monroe County @bellsouth.net); Anderson Family FH footer says © 2025; Graumlich, Owen, Lucas, Lumina and Hospice-NW emails were decoded from mailto/obfuscated page markup (they render normally to human visitors); Lucas's email was published on the family's affiliated familyowned.net facilities page rather than the primary domain; Lumina's source is a 2026 event page that may rotate; Calcutt-Dowling's published address uses the sister-firm domain @cainfuneralhome.com — that is how the firm itself lists it. Gaps: Delaware, Arkansas, Mississippi, Louisiana and Oklahoma yielded only contact-form sites and were skipped (quality over padding).
