Companion to the memorial list (`PARTNER_TARGETS.md`). Send copy: `PARTNER_OUTREACH_EMAILS_WEDDING.md`. Partners minted from this list get tokens **occasion-scoped to wedding** at `/support/partners`, referral links pointed at `wordsbywtm.com/wedding?ref=<token>`.

## How this list was built

Candidates were found via web search across four partner types that touch the reception toast — independent wedding planners, custom-ceremony officiants/celebrants, owner-operated wedding DJ/MCs, and family-owned boutique venues — and each organization's own website was then fetched directly. An email is included **only if it literally appears on the organization's own public site** (contact / about / team page) — no guessed address patterns, no directory or profile sites (The Knot, WeddingWire, Zola, Yelp were never used as an email source). The Source URL column is the exact page where the email appears. Chains and multi-op agencies were skipped (Wedgewood, venue-management groups, multi-DJ rosters); preference went to named owner-operators with a "toast/story" fit signal — celebrants who build ceremonies by gathering the couple's stories, MCs whose craft is running the toasts, planners whose services or blogs cover the reception program.

**Caveats on freshness (all emails seen live 2026-07-05):**
- Two addresses were decoded from Cloudflare email-protection markup (they render normally to human visitors): Boyden Farm `boydenevents@gmail.com`, Memphis Wedding DJ MA `markaustinanderson@gmail.com` — verify rendering in a browser before sending.
- Grace And Virtue Events was extracted from a `mailto:` link that may not render as visible text — verify visually (hence Tier C).
- Free-mail boxes published on the business's own site are common for small venues/celebrants/DJs (several gmail/yahoo below) — that is how the businesses list themselves; bounce-check the oldest-looking ones: `mastersofceremony@ymail.com` (Masters of Ceremony, TX), `ashley@boutiqueplanning.com` (site served over plain http; `brooke@` same page as backup).
- The Hive Wedding Collective's `anne@` is labeled "Media Requests and Other Inquiries" — the right channel for a partnership pitch, but not their couples line.
- Nicole Mower's address is displayed on-site in all caps; normalized to lowercase here.
- Elyse Jennings Weddings publishes a $200K wedding-budget minimum — ultra-luxury clientele; the courtesy discount will matter less there, the tool itself is the pitch.
- Shared studio mailboxes with a named principal (HappiLily `hello@`, Verdeaux `hello@`, Wedding Day Girl `info@`): address the named person in the body.
- Gaps: no NC/UT/NV/ID/MI/IA/MO-planner rows — candidates there were contact-form-only or failed verification (quality over padding). Verified spares are listed at the bottom.
