## How to send

1. Personalize the first line of Touch 1 with one specific detail about their business (their town, a real wedding on their blog, how they describe their MC style) before anything else.
2. **The 10–15/day cap is shared with the memorial track** — it's one hello@ mailbox and one domain. Count memorial + wedding sends together, spread through the morning.
3. Any reply, even a "not interested," gets a short human answer within a day; interested ones get their `?ref=` link plus the card page URL (wordsbywtm.com/partners/card?code=their-token) in the first response.
4. **Occasion scoping:** when minting the token at `/support/partners`, scope the partner to **wedding** (and anniversary too if they do vow renewals) and point their referral link at `wordsbywtm.com/wedding?ref=<token>` — a wedding partner's courtesy must not ride on other occasions.
5. **Never** frame the courtesy as money to the partner. It's a discount the *couple* (or whoever finalizes) receives; the partner arranges it. No commission, no revenue share, nothing to settle.
